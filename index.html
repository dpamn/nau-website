About Naman Agro Udhyog

Established in 1985, Naman Agro Udhyog is a renowned name in the agricultural machinery manufacturing sector, located in the heart of Shamli District, Uttar Pradesh. With nearly four decades of expertise, we have been committed to providing high-quality tractor parts and advanced farm equipment that enhance the productivity of farmers across India.

Our Expertise:

We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of tractor parts, tailored to support various field operations. Our precision-engineered parts include:

  • Hydraulic Lifts
  • Linkage Systems
  • Three-Point Hitch Components
  • PTO Shafts
  • Disc Plows
  • Cultivator Blades
  • Spray Pumps
In addition to tractor parts, Naman Agro Udhyog is a leading producer of tractor-operated spray machines, designed to meet the diverse needs of modern farming. Our sprayers are available in various tank capacities—500 litres, 1000 litres, and 2000 litres—ensuring that farmers can choose the best solution for their specific crop protection requirements. These sprayers are ideal for applying pesticides, herbicides, and fertilizers, offering uniform spray coverage and operational efficiency across various crop types. Why Choose Us: Quality and Durability: We use only the best materials and the latest technology to ensure our products are durable, reliable, and efficient. Tailored Solutions: Our wide range of tractor parts and sprayers are designed to meet the unique challenges of modern farming. Customer-Centric Approach: Since our inception, we have focused on building long-lasting relationships with our customers by providing personalized support and top-quality products. At Naman Agro Udhyog, we take pride in being a trusted partner for farmers, helping them optimize their agricultural operations with innovative, reliable, and affordable solutions.
